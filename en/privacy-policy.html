Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Information We Collect

This site (quick-tools.barofam.com) uses Google Analytics to collect statistical information such as the number of visitors, which pages are viewed, approximate location, and device type. We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name or email address.

Image Processing

Images you upload to the pixel art converter are processed entirely within your browser. They are never sent to or stored on any server.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish visitors. Visitors from the EU and UK are first shown a consent banner at the bottom of the page; if you choose "Reject," no cookies are stored and no statistics are collected. In other regions, analytics cookies may be used without a separate consent step. Your region is determined automatically based on your IP address, and your choice is saved in your browser so you are not asked again on future visits. You can reset your choice at any time by clearing cookies in your browser settings.

Advertising

Google AdSense ads may be added to this site in the future. When AdSense is enabled, additional cookies may be used to serve personalized ads, and this document will be updated accordingly.
