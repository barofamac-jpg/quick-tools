Quick Tools

A collection of small web tools that come in handy every day.

Elementary Learning Tools

  • ✖️ Multiplication Array Visualizer Shows multiplication as a dot array and teaches the commutative property.
  • 🎮 Multiplication Practice Game Practice multiplication by solving problems across 5 difficulty levels.
  • 🍕 Fraction Visualizer Shows fractions as pictures and lets you compare up to 6 at once.
  • 🍕 Fraction Practice Game Build your fraction sense by picking the bigger fraction, across 5 difficulty levels.
  • ⏱️ Study Timer Set your study and break time and watch progress on a circular clock face.

Image Converters

  • 🎨 Image → Pixel Art Converter Upload a photo and it's automatically turned into pixel art.
  • 🔄 Image Format & Size Converter Convert to PNG, JPG, or WebP and shrink the file size with a quality slider.
  • ✂️ Image Resize & Crop Tool Crop the area you want and resize it before downloading.
